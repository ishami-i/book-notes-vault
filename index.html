Book & Notes Vault is a lightweight personal catalog designed to help users store, organize, and track their reading and study materials in one place. The app allows you to record book titles, page counts, tags, and notes while providing smart search, filtering, and data summaries on your dashboard. It is built using vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, featuring localStorage data persistence, regex-based input validation, and accessible keyboard navigation for inclusive use.
